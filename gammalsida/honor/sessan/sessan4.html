"Sessan"

Sessan is a lovely cat that has a mind of her own. She is the first cat in the cattery that made the breeding possible.

Sessan is a quiet lady that sleeps most of the day or watching over the other cats. In the evening Sessan plays with the other cats and make sure that everyting is in order.

When ham or some other food is served she always first in place making her voice heard .

Sessan is a caring mother. She always take care of every kitten, including the other females kitten. You have to look hard to find a greater mum.

Unfortunatly nature has made sure she can not have any more babies, but sinse she isn´t neutered she stays on the female side.
